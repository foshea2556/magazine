This weeks theme: Disney princesses

ENCHANCTING FACTS ABOUT DISNEY PRINCESSES:

  • Snow White was 14 years old. She’s the youngest Disney Princess.
  • Pocahontas is the only princess based on a real person.
  • Jasmine is the second-youngest princess. She’s 15, one year older than Snow White.
  • Cinderella was 19.
  • Disney’s Pocahontas is 18 in the film but in real life, she was much younger when she met Jon Smith. Roy Disney said that it made a more dramatic telling of the story since they could work a romantic angle with an older character.
  • Merida is the only Disney Princess with a non-American accent.
